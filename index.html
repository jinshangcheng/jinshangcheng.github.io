Shangcheng Jin

I am a third-year PhD student at the Department of Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Guoliang Xing and Prof. Jianwei Huang. I also work closely with Prof. Jiayu Zhou.

My current research interests include machine learning for smart health systems and mobile sensing. I obtained my bachelor’s degree with honor (Rank 1/68) in Jul. 2019 from Information and Communication Engineering, Xiamen University.
